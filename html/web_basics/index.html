Rock on Garth, Party on Wayne

The art of becoming a rockstar is no walk in the park, it takes hard work, strong ethics and a lot of time and patience. Once you have mastered these 3 elements, you will soon be on the path to making millions of record sales and hundreds of thousands of loyal fans.

Disclaimer: becoming a rockstar does not guarantee success. Success is dependent on how you define it and how hard you work toward it. You should spend the majority of your time finding and strenghtening your team and practicing material you create as a unit.

For you to succeed, here are some tips for how to be safe and professional in the work environment!

  • Practice good hygiene.
  • Be emotionally aware of your mates.
  • Have the courage and the strength to carry through with any challenge.

A list of priorities you should consider before starting a band

  1. Am I doing this for money or the music?
  2. What is the end goal?
  3. What can I do to hype myself when the going gets rough?
